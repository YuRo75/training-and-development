1 The Microsoft Security portfolio

Microsoft Security is an integrated set of products that protect your whole digital estate and coordinate signals across it. The guiding principle is Zero Trust: verify explicitly, use least-privilege access, and assume breach.

Protect

Identity, devices, apps, email, and data.

Detect

Correlate signals across domains to find threats fast.

Respond

Investigate and remediate — increasingly with AI and automation.

2 Microsoft Defender XDR

Defender XDR (extended detection & response) unifies threat protection, consolidating signals across assets so you manage threats from one place — the Microsoft Defender portal.

ComponentProtects
Defender for EndpointLaptops, phones, servers — the endpoint pillar
Defender for IdentityMicrosoft Entra ID / on-prem identity threats
Defender for Office 365Email and collaboration (phishing, malware)
Defender for Cloud AppsSaaS app usage and risk
One incident queueDefender XDR correlates related alerts into a single incident with the full attack story, instead of a flood of disconnected signals.

3 Microsoft Sentinel

Microsoft Sentinel is the cloud-native SIEM. It ingests security data from across your environment — cloud and on-prem — for detection, hunting, and AI-driven defense.

  • Data lake — a managed, security-purpose-built lake for scale and retention.
  • KQL analytics — hunt and detect across all your data.
  • Sentinel graph — visualize blast radius and attack paths.
  • Integrates with Defender XDR for a complete XDR + SIEM solution.

4 Threat hunting with KQL

Both Defender advanced hunting and Sentinel use KQL (Kusto Query Language). The mental model: start from a table, then pipe (|) it through filters and aggregations.

// Top devices running powershell.exe in the last 7 days
DeviceProcessEvents
| where Timestamp > ago(7d)
| where FileName == "powershell.exe"
| summarize Runs = count() by DeviceName
| top 10 by Runs
OperatorDoes
whereFilter rows by a condition
projectPick / rename columns
summarizeAggregate (count, sum, avg) by groups
joinCombine two tables on a key
bin() / renderBucket by time and chart it
top / takeLimit results
Filter earlyPut your tightest where (especially a time filter like ago(1d)) first — it makes queries dramatically faster and cheaper.

6 Purview (data) & Intune (devices)

Microsoft Purview

Governs and protects data: classification, sensitivity labels, DLP, insider-risk, and compliance — across Microsoft 365 and beyond.

Microsoft Intune

Manages and secures devices and apps; enforces the compliance that Conditional Access can require before granting access.
Labels travelA Purview sensitivity label stays with the content — into exports, Copilot answers, and shared files — enforcing protection wherever data goes.

7 Zero Trust principles

PrincipleMeaning
Verify explicitlyAuthenticate and authorize on all available signals
Least-privilege accessLimit access with just-in-time, just-enough rights
Assume breachSegment, encrypt, and monitor as if attackers are inside
Defense in depthEntra verifies identity, Intune attests the device, Defender watches the endpoint, Purview protects the data, and Sentinel ties it all together.
